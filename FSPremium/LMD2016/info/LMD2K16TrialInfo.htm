Prices / Online Order

LMD Innovative products can be purchased online or from international vendors listed below.

Online orders are processed by ShareIt!. The online credit card processing is done via an encrypted Internet commerce system, so that your credit card information is protected. In addition ShareIt! supports additional payment options like VISA, Eurocard/MasterCard, American Express or Diners Club credit card support.

US and Canadian customers may also order by calling 1-800-903-4152. In the pricelists below direct links to ShareIt! order pages are available. If you have any problems or if you don't want to order via ShareIt! - please contact us directly and we will help you ASAP.

To review prices in other currencies (based on the current exchange rate) check LMD online shop at ShareIt! and visit the order page of a specific product.
All prices in € (EURO). Additional 19% VAT applies for german residents/companies and EU residents/companies without valid VAT-ID.

 After purchase access data (usernames and password) for your personal protected download area on our web site is and provided. In these protected download areas you can download each available and most current version for all supported compilers of your LMD product.
Keep this registration utility at a safe place - you will require it for any free update in the future. If you should lose these information, please contact sales@lmd.de mentioning your serial# or order number. We resend personal access data again.
